Software Carpentry began in 1998 as a week-long training course at Los Alamos National Laboratory. With support from the Python Software Foundation, we released Version 3 of our materials under an open license in 2004, and in 2010, with support from several new sponsors, we produced the online video tutorials of Version 4. Our main focus now is intensive two-day boot camps, and we hope to release a new version of our material in 2013.
