Sophia Clay, VP is an Agile Engineer and Technology Coach at JPMorgan Chase, where she works directly with Application Development teams to drive the adoption of Agile Software Development Principles and Best Practices. Sophia has a BS in Computer Science and an MS in Business Administration and Information Technology. She has experience as an Application Developer, Application Architect, Development Team lead and writing and deploying enterprise applications using C++, JAVA/J2EE, JavaScript and Web Services. In addition to working at JPMorgan Chase, Sophia is an active member of Agile Alliance, IEEE Computer Society and Women in Technology.
