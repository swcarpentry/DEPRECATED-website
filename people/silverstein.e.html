Elenita Silverstein, ED co-heads the global Quantitative Research Analytics Team at JPMorgan Chase. In her role she directs (and provides hands-on support in) projects using cutting edge computing technology involving High Performance Computing, C++, Python, Java, C# and Web Servers. The team is responsible for software and hardware infrastructure for the strategic quantitative and pricing library used in all of JPMorgan Chase risk systems. She has a MS in Computer Science from Rutgers University and a BS in Computer Science from Ateneo de Manila University. She is also a busy mother of two and holds a second degree black belt in karate (working on her third).
